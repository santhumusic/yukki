Commands List

New to Yukki Music Bot and wanna see all the commands and their usages? You are in the right place!

/play - Add a song to the queue and play it [Audio Only].

Usage:
/play [song title] - plays the first result from Youtube
/play [URL] - plays the provided song, playlist, stream m3u8 link, spotify music, apple music track or soundcloud

Examples:
/play Havana
/play https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HCjNJDNzw8Y

/vplay - Add a song to the queue and play it [Video+Audio Only].

Usage:
/vplay [song title] - plays the first result from Youtube
/vplay [URL] - plays the provided song, playlist, stream m3u8 link, spotify music, apple music track or soundcloud

Examples:
/vplay Havana
/vplay https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HCjNJDNzw8Y

/cplay - Add a song to the queue and play it [Channel Play].

Usage:

Examples:
/cplay Havana

/playforce - Stop the current playing music and start playing searced track.

Force Play stops the current playing track on voice chat and starts playing the searched track instantly without disturbing/clearing queue.

Other Similar Commands : /playforce or /vplayforce or /cplayforce or /cvplayforce

Usage:
/playforce [song title] - plays the first result from Youtube
/playforce [URL] - plays the provided song, playlist, stream m3u8 link, spotify music, apple music track or soundcloud

Examples:
/playforce Havana
/playforce https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HCjNJDNzw8Y

/channelplay - Connect your channel to group.

Connect channel to a group and stream music on channel's voice chat from your group.

1. Get your channel username or chat_id
2. Goto your group from where you want to play music on channel
3. Give command: /channeplay [username or id]

Usage:
/channeplay [username or id]

Examples:
/channeplay @TheYukki

Playlist Commands - Play served caved playlists

Usage:
/playlist - Check Your Saved Playlist On Servers.
/deleteplaylist - Delete any saved music in your playlist
/play - Start playing Your Saved Playlist from Servers.
